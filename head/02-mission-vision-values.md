# Fjord Anglers — Mission, Vision & Values

## Mission (what we do, for whom, today)
We provide anglers from Central Europe the best experience of nature and fishing in Nordic countries.

## Vision (3–5 years out)
To be the biggest provider of guided fishing trips in the Nordic market, and eventually the world's biggest agency for fly fishing travel — not sea fishing, but shore fishing worldwide.

## Values (what we refuse to compromise on)

- **Care for nature** — we earn this by *telling the story of Nordic waters*, not just claiming to love them. We publish on the real issues — salmon collapse, sea lice, invasive species, climate pressure — and bring our community into the fight. Content-led now; concrete partnerships and material commitments next.

- **Curated quality** — every guide, river, and lodge is one we'd book ourselves. We say no to what isn't good enough rather than pad the catalog. Premium not as luxury — premium as *we did the work so you don't have to*.

- **Authentic experience** — we sell the trip we'd take, not the trip that markets best. Founders' faces visible, real spots, real conditions, no staged shots.

- **Real talk** — no marketing BS. Straight with our community about what works, what doesn't, and what we're still figuring out. This is the voice — keep it even as we scale.
