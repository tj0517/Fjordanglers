# Fjord Anglers — Product & Customers

## Product in one paragraph
We provide custom guided trip experiences to anglers — connecting passionate fishers in Central Europe with local guides in Scandinavia for shore and fly fishing adventures.

## How it works (step by step)
1. User fills out the request form on the website
2. We review the request and contact the guide
3. We send the user an offer
4. User accepts the offer
5. The deal is done

## Who it's for

| Persona | Who they are | What they want |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary** | Experienced angler | Amazing trips with pure nature and guides who do their best to catch the angler's dream fish |
| **Secondary** | Angler with family / Amateur angler | Experience something new, discover new places with local guides, great atmosphere, and better chances of catching fish |

## What we explicitly DON'T do
- We are **not** a travel agency
- We are **not** a marketplace — we build a **brand**, not just a listing platform

## Positioning statement
> For passionate anglers, we are the place that offers the best fishing guided trips with local guides in the Scandinavian market.
