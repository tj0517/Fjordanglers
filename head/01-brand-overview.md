## Origin Story
Fjord Anglers was created from a love of exploring through fishing. The founders are a group of students from Poland in their 20s who love to travel to Nordic countries like Norway, Sweden, and Iceland — traveling with rod to explore nature and catch the fish hidden in the best spots of each country.
