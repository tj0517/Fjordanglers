## Tagline
**Wild Nordic. Real guides.**

Rationale: *"Wild"* echoes the universal forum word in EN/DE/PL voice-of-customer data. *"Nordic"* anchors the brand to its geography. *"Real guides"* names the strongest content lever (Pillar 4 — Behind the Guides) and the biggest finding from VOC research: customer praise tracks individual guide names. Two of the five brand personality adjectives (*Wild*, *Real*) carried in four words.

**Hero subhead (paired):** *"We've fished it. That's why we book it."* — captures the curation promise in founder voice.

## Metrics we track

### North-star metric
**Inquiry-form submissions per week (weighted by source quality).**

Why: it's the single bottleneck where brand love converts into revenue. IG followers are vanity until they convert; site traffic is vanity until it inquires. Source-weighting prevents low-quality bot/spam form fills from inflating the number.

Targets to set per quarter once campaign data starts flowing.

### Supporting metrics

| Metric | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Instagram followers | Brand-first approach: demand exists on the market, but we need to build trust before we sell |
| Requested trips | Direct revenue driver |
| User time on site | Indicates the marketing flow works and the site holds interest |
| New users on site | Proves our ads and organic marketing are working |
