# Welcome to FjordAnglers
## Guide Setup Guide

> Your account is active. This guide walks you through everything you need to set up before you start receiving bookings.

---

## 1. Log in to your dashboard

Go to **fjordanglers.com/dashboard**

If you haven't set a password yet, click **"Forgot password"** on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a link to set your password.

---

## 2. Set up payouts — how you get paid

This is the most important step. You cannot receive money without completing it.

Go to: **Dashboard → Account**

There are two options depending on your country.

---

### Option A — Stripe Connect (Norway, Sweden, Finland, Denmark, Germany…)

This is the standard option. Stripe handles all card payments and transfers money directly to your bank account every week.

**How to set it up:**

1. Click **"Connect with Stripe"**
2. You'll be redirected to Stripe's website
3. Fill in your personal details:
   - Full name (as on your ID)
   - Date of birth
   - Home address
   - Bank account number (IBAN)
   - Upload a photo of your ID or passport
4. Submit and return to FjordAnglers

Stripe will review your account — this usually takes **1–2 business days**. You'll see the status change to **"Active — payouts enabled"** once it's done.

**What happens after Stripe is active:**
- Anglers pay a 30% deposit online when they book
- You receive the money every Monday for the previous week's completed trips
- You collect the remaining 70% yourself on the day of the trip (cash or card, your choice)

---

### Option B — Bank transfer / IBAN (Iceland, Croatia)

Stripe is not available in Iceland or Croatia. Anglers pay a small platform fee online, and you collect your guide fee directly.

**How to set it up:**

Go to **Dashboard → Account → Payouts → Bank transfer tab**

Fill in:
- First and last name
- Date of birth
- Home address
- Your IBAN (bank account number)
- BIC / SWIFT code (optional)
- Bank name (optional)

Save the form. FjordAnglers will transfer your earnings manually after each completed trip.

---

## 3. Set up how anglers pay you

Go to: **Dashboard → Account → Accepted payment methods**

After a booking is confirmed, anglers need to pay the remaining balance (70%) before the trip. Choose how you want to collect it:

| Option | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Online (card)** | Angler pays by card via the platform before the trip |
| **Cash** | Angler brings cash on the day of the trip |
| **Both** | Angler can choose |

**Recommendation:** Select **"Both"** — it gives anglers more flexibility and reduces cancellations.

---

## 4. Create your first trip

Go to: **Dashboard → My Trips → New Trip**

A trip is the fishing experience you offer. You can have multiple trips (e.g. "Half day salmon fly fishing", "3-day pike expedition", etc.).

Fill in the following:

**Basic info:**
- **Title** — the name of the trip as anglers will see it
- **Description** — what to expect, what makes this trip special
- **Location** — country, city/region, and GPS coordinates if possible
- **Season** — which months you run this trip (e.g. April – October)

**Trip details:**
- **Duration** — half day (4h), full day (8h), or multi-day; you can add multiple options
- **Price** — per person, in EUR
- **Max guests** — how many anglers fit per trip
- **Difficulty** — All Levels / Intermediate / Expert
- **Target species** — which fish you're targeting
- **Technique** — fly fishing, spinning, lure fishing, etc.
- **Catch & release** — yes or no

**What's included:**
Tick everything that's included in the price:
- Fishing rods, tackle, bait
- Boat
- Safety equipment
- Fishing license
- Lunch, drinks
- Fish cleaning
- Transport, accommodation

**Booking type — important:**

| Type | What it means |
|------|---------------|
| **Direct booking** | Angler picks a date and pays the deposit immediately. You confirm or decline within 48h. |
| **Request only** | Angler sends a request with their dates. You review it and send a custom offer with price and dates. |
| **Both** | Angler can choose either method. |

**Add photos** — at least 1 photo is required. The first photo becomes the cover image. Good photos = more bookings.

When everything is filled in, click **"Publish"**. Your trip will appear on fjordanglers.com.

---

## 5. Set up your availability calendar

Go to: **Dashboard → Calendar**

The calendar shows your availability and existing bookings month by month. Set it up so anglers only see dates when you're actually available.

### Block specific days or periods

Click on any day to open the day details. From there you can:
- **Block a single day** — e.g. a day you're already booked privately
- **Block a date range** — e.g. a holiday or a period when you're unavailable

To block multiple days at once, click **"Select days"** in the top right corner, pick the days, and then click **"Block selected"**.

### Set your weekly schedule

Click **"Block"** → **"Weekly schedule"** to set recurring unavailable days. For example, if you never guide on Sundays and Mondays, set those as blocked every week for a given period.

This is the most efficient way to manage availability — you set it once per season.

### Reading the calendar

- **White** — available
- **Orange** — day you've selected/confirmed for a booking
- **Blue** — angler's requested dates
- **Red strikethrough** — your off-day (you can still manually override)
- **Grey** — past / unavailable

---

## 6. How bookings and requests work

Go to: **Dashboard → Bookings & Requests**

Everything comes into one place — both direct bookings and custom requests. There are three tabs:

### Tab: Needs Action 🔴

This is where you need to respond. You'll see it highlighted with an orange dot when something needs your attention.

**For a direct booking (orange "Pending" badge):**
1. Click on the booking
2. Review the angler's details, dates, and group size
3. Choose **Accept** or **Decline**
4. If you accept: select the confirmed dates from the calendar and add a personal message (optional)
5. If you decline: explain why (optional) and suggest alternative dates (optional)
6. Submit — the angler is notified automatically

After acceptance, the angler receives a payment link for the 30% deposit. The booking moves to **"Accepted"** until they pay, then to **"Confirmed"**.

---

**For a custom request (purple "Request" badge):**
1. Click on the request
2. Read the angler's message, dates, group size, and preferences
3. On the right side, you'll see a chat — you can message the angler directly before deciding
4. When you're ready, click **"Send Offer"**:
   - Set the price
   - Choose the dates
   - Add a message
5. The angler receives your offer and can accept or decline it
6. If they accept → they pay the deposit → booking is **Confirmed**

---

### Tab: Upcoming

All confirmed trips that haven't happened yet. These are bookings where the deposit has been paid.

From here you can:
- View angler contact details and notes
- See deposit and remaining balance status
- Mark the remaining balance as paid (if collected in cash)
- Open the chat to send trip preparation info

---

### Tab: Past

All completed, cancelled, and declined bookings. This is your history.

---

## 7. Collecting the remaining balance (70%)

After the trip is confirmed (deposit paid), the remaining balance is due before the trip.

**If you selected "Online" payments:**
Go to the booking → click **"Request balance payment"**. The angler receives a payment link and pays by card. You'll see the status update when it's done.

**If you selected "Cash" payments:**
Collect the cash on the day. Then go to the booking and click **"Mark balance as paid"** to confirm it in the system.

---

## 8. Other account settings

Go to: **Dashboard → Account**

### Photo & marketing consent

If you turn this **on**, FjordAnglers may use your photos on the website, Instagram, and in ads. Your name will always be credited. You can change this at any time.

### Listing visibility

If you need to temporarily hide your profile (e.g. fully booked for the season, personal reasons), turn this **off**. Your profile and trips disappear from search results. Existing bookings are **not** affected — they continue normally.

To go live again, simply turn visibility back **on**.

---

## 9. Your plan and commission

Go to: **Dashboard → Account → Your plan**

FjordAnglers charges a commission on each confirmed booking:
- **Standard rate: 10%** of the booking total
- **Founding Guide rate: 8%** — if you were among the first guides to join, you have this lower rate for your first 24 months

The commission is deducted automatically before your payout. You always see your net payout amount in the booking details.
