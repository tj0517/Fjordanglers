# Fjord Anglers — Market, Competition & Business Model

## Geography
Primary region: **Central Europe**

## Competitors

### FishingBooker (fishingbooker.com)
- **Strengths:** Scale — 1000+ guides worldwide
- **Weaknesses:** Just a simple marketplace, no brand
- **Our edge:** They focus on the USA, not Scandinavia

### Rod Society (rodsociety.com)
- **Strengths:** Great brand across their offering — inspiration for our business model
- **Weaknesses:** Poor-looking website, underused social potential
- **Our edge:** Similar business model but in Scandinavia — they only cover Italy, France, Spain, and Argentina

## How we make money
Commission on every guided experience sold through our site.

## Revenue streams
1. Commission on trips (primary)
2. Brand deals with gear shops featured on the site (future)

## Unit economics

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Average transaction value | €500 |
| Our take rate / fee | €100 |
| Target CPA | €25 |

## Pricing / commission rules
- We know the net price the guide wants per trip; we set the angler-facing offer price so that **(offer price − net price) = our commission = our revenue**
- Once we're driving traffic, we'll open brand partnerships to showcase top fishing gear on the site
